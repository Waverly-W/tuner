Chapter 1: The Beginning

The sun rose over the horizon, casting a golden glow on the sleepy village. Harry rubbed his eyes and sat up in bed. "Today is the day," he whispered to himself.

He looked out the window. The birds were singing. It felt like a good omen.

Chapter 2: The Journey

Harry packed his bag with essentials. A map, a compass, and a sandwich. He wasn't sure where he was going, but he knew he had to leave.

"Goodbye, home," he said softly, closing the door behind him. The road ahead was long, but he was ready.
